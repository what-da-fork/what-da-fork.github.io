Profile

I build security automation and AI systems grounded in deep detection engineering experience. After starting in IT administration and taking ownership of security practices, I moved into detection engineering and learned Python, cloud engineering, infrastructure as code, and CI/CD to remove the recurring bottlenecks around deployment, validation, tuning, and investigations. I now operate as both a hands-on builder and architect, with 9+ years across IT and security and 7+ years in cybersecurity. I work backward from the end-state architecture, think across upstream and downstream effects, and ship reusable increments that solve immediate problems without creating future rework.
